####NUSwan
I am currently working on the command and control system of [NUSwan](http://arl.nus.edu.sg/twiki6/bin/view/ARL/Swan) (pronounced as <i>new-swan</i>), an autonomous 
surface vehicle (ASV) platform for monitoring freshwater reservoirs in Singapore. These robotic swans measure and 
various physical and biological parameters to a cloud storage so that the water quality can be analysed in real-time.
