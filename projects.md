####NUSwan
I am currently working on the command and control system of [NUSwan](http://arl.nus.edu.sg/twiki6/bin/view/ARL/Swan) (pronounced as <i>new-swan</i>),
a team of autonomous surface vehicle (ASV) platform for monitoring freshwater bodies
in Singapore. These robotic swans measure and send various physical and biological parameters
of water to a central cloud storage in real-time. Thus a cooperative
team provides both temporal and spatial water sampling capability. This helps us to keep
track of water quality parameters such as harmful algal blooms (HABs) that affects
the health of a water body's fauna.<br/>
