About me

Hi there! I am Ashish Raste, a research engineer at the Acoustic Research Lab, Tropical Marine Science Institute in Singapore. My research interests include collaborative path planning of autonomous vehicles and underwater acoustic networks.

I enjoy playing badminton and cricket. I also like spending time on practicing the guitar chords, cooking Indian food recipes and reading books where I have a strong bias towards science fiction and astronomy.

In this blog space, I look forward to write about my experiments in the field of robotics. Occasionally they might get mingled with my random thought experiments.
