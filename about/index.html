About me

Hi there! I am Ashish, a robotics software engineer at Dorabot Inc. in Shenzhen, China. I work on multi-robot transportation system for transporting packages in a warehouse environment.

Previously I was with Acoustic Research Lab, Tropical Marine Science Institute in Singapore, where I worked on the onboard-autonomy software of marine robots.

I like spending time cooking Indian food recipes and reading books, with a strong bias towards science fiction and astronomy. Recently, I've been fascinated by Mandarin Chinese language, and am making a steady progress in learning the language's intricacies. 我会说一点儿中文 :)

In this blog space, I look forward to write and share my experiences related to the field of robotics.
