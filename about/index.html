About me

Hi there, welcome to my blog!

I am Ashish, a Software Engineer passionate about robotics and machine vision. I work at BHP as a Tech Lead. I was previously a Robotics Engineer at Dorabot in Shenzhen China and at Acoustic Research Laboratory in Singapore working on the onboard-autonomy software of mobile warehouse robots and marine robots respectively.

I enjoy reading books in my free time. I also like learning new languages when possible, and have recently picked up Mandarin Chinese after being fascinated by its origins and evolution. 我会说一点儿点儿普通话 :)
