Robust Range Only Beacon Localization Talk

At the Papers We Love SG Chapter meetup that is held monthly in Singapore, we discussed about the robot localization technique for underwater vehicles as worked out by the authors of this paper.

Firstly I will log some important feedback that I gathered from my friends and myself after presenting the paper:

  1. I need at least two to three days for preparing the slides where I should make sure to rehearse at least two or three times.
  2. For any talk involving robots I should take care, wherever possible, to explain the concepts and implementations through simulated videos or real-robot demos.
  3. When we are foreseeing few technical jargons that are soon to be talked about, it's better to add some relevant humor before or after discussing them which usually helps the audience to grasp them quickly.
  4. Finally, I shall never present anything with an empty stomach.
